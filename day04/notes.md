## Day 04 Thoughts: 

- The dominant spectral peaks correspond to the carrier frequency components at ±fc. Additional smaller spectral components appeared due to phase transitions introduced by the BPSK symbol sequence. These sidebands represent the information-bearing part of the modulated signal.

- Under AWGN conditions, the BPSK carrier peaks remained visible in the frequency domain, while broadband noise raised the spectral floor. In time domain, carrier  waveform experienced random amplitude perbutation , making it more difficult to detect symbol.
