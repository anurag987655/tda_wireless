# Topology: A general introduction

- Topology is study of shapes that remains unchanged during continuous deformation. 
- In topology a coffee mug and a donought are topologically equivalent. 
- In more general term, topology allows the shape to be bend, twist or deform without tearing 

# Connected Components
A connected component is a piece that stays together. for example a simple circle is one connected component.

# Loops
A loop is a closed path. It starts and end at same point however crossover is not allowed.

# Holes
A hole is a place where something is missing which prevent the surface from collapsing.
