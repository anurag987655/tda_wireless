# Day 09 : Experiment Log

## Experiment 01 : 3D Delay Embedding of Sine Wave
- Clean, smooth shape.
- Forms an ellipse-like structure, similar to what was observed in the 2D delay embedding.

## Experiment 02 : 3D Delay Embedding of Noisy Sine Wave
- The shape is retained, however it almost resembles a donut (torus) structure.

## Experiment 03 : 2D and 3D Delay Embedding of Square Wave
- The 2D embedding formed 4 distinct point clusters.
- The 3D embedding formed clusters almost at the vertices of a unit cube.

## Experiment 04 : Noisy Square Wave
- The noise forms a dense cloud of clusters near the original embedded points of the square wave.
